NAGESH ALURI

National Museum

It holds a variety of articles ranging from pre-historic era to modern works of art. The museum has around 200,000 works of art, mostly Indian, but some of foreign origin, covering over 5,000 years.It also houses the National Museum Institute of History of Art Conservation and Museology on the first floor which was established in 1983 and has been to be a university since 1989, and runs Masters and Doctoral level courses in art history, conservation and museology.
